## Methodology & Caveats

**Data source:** Each school's official athletics website, 2025 men's soccer roster page.

**Important timing caveat:** This is the 2025 fall roster. Players listed as Sr. or Gr. have already graduated. The 2026 fall roster will have:
- All 2025 freshmen now sophomores
- All 2025 sophomores now juniors
- All 2025 juniors now seniors *(rising seniors)*
- New 2026 freshman class (often 5-10 players plus transfer portal grads)

**Opportunity score** is a heuristic, not a guarantee. Coaches actively recruit transfer-portal players to fill graduating slots. Platform Sports Management should confirm with each coach:
1. *How many central midfielders are in your 2026 recruiting class?*
2. *Of your 2025 sophomores and juniors at MF, how many do you project as 2026 starters?*
3. *What's the depth chart you're building toward 2026?*
